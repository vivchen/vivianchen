About

I am a Front-End Web Developer and Web Designer currently residing in Vancouver. I am passionate about clean, semantic, organized markup that eliminates excess code.

I approach design problems with a passion for usability and user interaction. I love to create interfaces that promote ease of use and optimize how customers interact with products and features.

When I'm not building projects I like to spend my spare time rock climbing, practicing yoga and playing piano.

I am currently seeking out career opportunities, if you think we'd be a good match I'd love to chat.
